\subsection{Firm-Level Fatal Pivot}
Firm-level $\mathrm{dim4}$ predicts 5--7 year collapse with ROC-AUC $= 0.996$
($N = 83$ firms).

\subsection{Threshold Invariants}

  \item $\mathrm{dim4} > 1.0$: irreversible band (36 countries above)
